Transparent Charities Accountable to YOU

With the Ethereum blockchain finally charitable organizations can be created with transparent accounting,
so you can donate to your favorite cause and know EXACTLY where your donation went and what it accomplished.

Saving the World Together

The Giveth system enables accountability on chain and off! Every person that touches your donation has an open line of communication where you two can connect. The goal is to build a community around a cause; so non-monetary resources can be shares as well.

Rewarding Good People's Good Deeds

The Giveth smart contracts, can reduce the trust required to support your favorite cause. Instead of donating, and then hoping it produces outcomes, the donation can be locked into milestones, released to the charity for results, incentivizing them to continue the good work.

Permissionless innovation

We are completely opensource. We invite anyone to contribute and build on top of our platform. Developers can take our base line system as a launching pad to innovate further. Imagine starting a decentralized charity to build an orphanage while testing an experimental governance system for allocating donations. Live smart contract testing while making the world a better place.
